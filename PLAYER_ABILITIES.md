# Player Abilities Reference

This document lists each character's attack, super, gadget, and notable traits.

## Attack Types (Glossary)

- standard: Single straight projectile.
- spread: 3-projectile fan spread.
- triple: Wide 3-projectile fan with full per-shot damage.
- double: Two parallel projectiles.
- heavy: Slow, high-damage projectile (often explosive).
- rapid: Fast burst cadence (double-tap style).
- homing: Projectile steers toward nearest enemy.
- boomerang: Projectile returns after a short time.
- aoehit: Projectile that also deals area damage.
- venomshot: Fast projectile that applies poison ticks.
- pumpburst: Short-range 4-projectile fan burst.

## Water-Walk Trait

The following characters can move over water tiles (water no longer blocks movement for them):

- Fidas
- Kleftros
- Kapnas
- Tornader
